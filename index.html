Dengue Awareness

Stay informed. Stay safe. Protect against dengue fever.

What is Dengue Fever?

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection caused by the bite of the Aedes aegypti mosquito. It spreads rapidly in tropical and subtropical climates worldwide. Severe dengue can be life-threatening.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Severe headache
  • Pain behind the eyes
  • Muscle and joint pains
  • Nausea and vomiting

  • Mild bleeding (nose or gums)

Prevention Tips

  • Use mosquito repellents and nets
  • Eliminate stagnant water around your home
  • Wear protective clothing
  • Ensure proper waste disposal
  • Community awareness campaigns

Treatment & First Aid

There is no specific cure for dengue, but supportive care is essential:

  • Stay hydrated (drink plenty of fluids)
  • Use paracetamol for fever and pain (avoid aspirin/ibuprofen)
  • Rest in a cool, mosquito-free environment
  • Seek immediate medical attention if symptoms worsen (severe abdominal pain, bleeding, difficulty breathing)
